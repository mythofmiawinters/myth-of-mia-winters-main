WHO ARE WE?

We are an electronic post-hardcore hybrid band based in Castroville, TX, inspired by Coheed & Cambria and Alexisonfire. New music and shows are coming soon near you in San Antonio! :D

  • Brenden Riker - Guitar, Harsh Vocals

  • George Gonzales - Synth and Electronics
